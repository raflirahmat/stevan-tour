ITINERARY:

DAY 1 – Labuan Bajo → Ende → Moni Village (L/D)

Your journey begins with a morning pick-up from your hotel in Labuan Bajo. You will be transferred to the airport for your flight to Ende (flight ticket optional depending on package).

Upon arrival in Ende, our guide and driver will welcome you and begin the drive toward Moni Village—a peaceful mountain village located at the base of Mount Kelimutu. The drive takes approximately 1.5–2 hours with beautiful scenery of valleys, rice terraces, and local villages.

Along the way, stop by a local restaurant for lunch. Upon arrival in Moni, check in to your eco-lodge or mountain hotel and enjoy the cool highland air. Spend the afternoon exploring the village, visiting local craft shops, or relaxing at the hotel.

In the evening, enjoy dinner at the lodge/restaurant.

DAY 2 – Sunrise at Kelimutu Crater Lakes → Ende → Labuan Bajo (B/L)

Wake up early at around 04:00 AM and drive to the Kelimutu National Park gate. From here, continue with a short hike of about 20–25 minutes to the summit viewpoint.

Arrive at the top just in time for sunrise over the Three-Colored Lakes—a magical moment as the colors shift with the morning light. Enjoy the peaceful atmosphere, panoramic views, and cool morning air that make Kelimutu one of the most unforgettable destinations in Flores.

After taking photos and exploring the viewpoint, return to Moni for breakfast. Then check out and drive back to Ende.

Lunch will be served at a local restaurant before heading to the airport for your flight back to Labuan Bajo.

Upon arrival in Labuan Bajo, our team will transfer you back to your hotel.
