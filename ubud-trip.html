Ubud Trip

10 hours • Pickup From Bali Area

Discover the enchanting beauty of Ubud, where traditional culture meets natural wonders. This immersive tour takes you through Ubud’s lush landscapes, sacred temples, and breathtaking viewpoints. Walk through the vibrant Monkey Forest, admire the Tegalalang Rice Terrace, and experience the famous Bali Swing for an adrenaline-filled moment. End your adventure with a visit to the sacred Tirta Empul Temple, where locals perform purification rituals, and enjoy a free Luwak Coffee tasting voucher, a unique coffee experience you won’t forget.

Highlights:

  • Stroll through the Monkey Forest, home to playful Balinese long-tailed monkeys
  • Witness the beauty of Tegalalang Rice Terrace, a UNESCO-recognized cultural landscape
  • Feel the rush of Bali Swing, soaring above the jungle for stunning panoramic views
  • Visit Tirta Empul Temple, known for its sacred purification pools
  • Savor the taste of Luwak Coffee, one of the world’s rarest and most luxurious coffee experiences
