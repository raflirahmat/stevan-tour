Overview

Embark on a journey through Bali’s cultural and natural wonders with our Tanah Lot Temple and Scenic Highlands Tour. This tour blends spiritual heritage and breathtaking landscapes, taking you to ancient temples, cascading waterfalls, and lush rice terraces. Visit the iconic Tanah Lot Temple, admire the beauty of Taman Ayun, refresh yourself at Leke-Leke Waterfall, and marvel at the stunning Jatiluwih Rice Terrace, a UNESCO-recognized site showcasing Bali’s traditional farming system.

  • Walk through the endless green landscapes of Jatiluwih Rice Terrace, one of Bali’s most scenic rice fields
