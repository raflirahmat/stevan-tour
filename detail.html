PINK BEACH

Pink beach is located near by komodo island. This place have a very nice pink beach, crystal water, and also beautiful under water marine life. Pink beach is a one of the nice spot to do snorkeling on the komodo island area. We can do snorkeling and also relax time right here.

KOMODO ISLAND

Komodo island is the biggest island in the komodo national park. Here we will see the komodo dragons. Beside the komodo dragons, we will see the others kind of the animals. There is timor deer, buffalo, wild boar, etc.

TAKA MAKASAR

Taka makasar is a very small island that only consists of the white sand. The clear water and white sand. The view of this small island are also very best things that must be visited. In additional to enjoying the beach also do the snorkeling. This place also we can snorkeling with baby shark and turtles.

MANTA POINT

Manta point is located near by taka makasar island. This place is very highly recommended for the snorkeler people who loves to do the snorkeling with manta rays.

SEBAYUR ISLAND

Sebayur island is indeed a snorkeling spot! It’s known for its vibrant coral reefs and diverse marine life, making it an excellent destination for snorkeling and diving. The island is very clear water with colourful fish, coral, and another marine creatures.
