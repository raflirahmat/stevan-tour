Day 1 –bali – Labuan Bajo – Kelor Island – Manjarite – Kalong Island (L/D)

Upon arrival in Labuan Bajo, our team will pick you up from the airport or your hotel and transfer you to the harbor to begin your sailing adventure.

Your first stop is Kelor Island, where you can enjoy a short hike for panoramic hilltop views or snorkel in crystal-clear waters. Afterward, we continue to Manjarite, a calm and shallow snorkeling spot with vibrant coral gardens — perfect for relaxing underwater time.

As the day ends, the boat sails to Kalong Island, where thousands of flying foxes soar out of the mangrove forest at sunset. This natural spectacle is one of Komodo’s highlights. Dinner and overnight on board.

Day 2 – Padar Island – Pink Beach – Komodo Island – Taka Makasar – Manta Point (B/L/D)

We start early today. Before sunrise, you’ll trek to the top viewpoint of Padar Island, one of the most iconic landscapes in Indonesia. The sunrise here is unforgettable.

After the hike, the boat heads to Pink Beach, where rose-tinted sand meets turquoise water — perfect for snorkeling, swimming, or relaxing.

Next, we visit Komodo Island for a guided trek with park rangers to see the famous Komodo dragons in their natural habitat.

From there, the journey continues to Taka Makasar, a tiny crescent-shaped sandbar surrounded by clear blue water, followed by Manta Point, where you will try your chance to snorkel with graceful manta rays. Dinner and overnight on board.

Day 3 – Siaba Island – Kanawa Island – Return to Labuan Bajo (B/L)

This morning, we visit Siaba Island, often called “Turtle City” due to the frequent sightings of sea turtles swimming calmly around the reefs. Enjoy snorkeling in its calm and colorful underwater world.

Next, the boat sails to Kanawa Island, known for its shallow coral beds and crystal-clear waters — a peaceful final stop before heading back to the harbor.

Day 4 – Labuan Bajo → Ende (Flight) → Moni Village (B/L)

After breakfast, we transfer you to Komodo Airport for your short flight to Ende. Once you arrive, your local guide will welcome you and start the journey to Moni Village, a peaceful highland area at the foot of Mount Kelimutu.

Along the way, we stop at the beautiful Blue Stone Beach (Penggajawa), famous for its unique blue-green pebbles lining the shore. After enjoying the scenery, continue to Ende town for lunch at a local restaurant.

The drive to Moni offers rice terraces, mountain views, and villages along the slopes. Arrive in Moni in the late afternoon and check in to your lodge. Rest early for tomorrow’s sunrise excursion.

Day 5 – Kelimutu Sunrise – Ende Airport Departure (B)

Wake up early (around 04:00) and drive to Kelimutu National Park. From the parking area, a short walk brings you to the summit viewpoint.

After enjoying the sunrise, return to your hotel in Moni for breakfast. When you’re ready, we drive back to Ende Airport for your departure flight. Tour ends with incredible memories of Komodo’s ocean beauty and Kelimutu’s highland wonders.
