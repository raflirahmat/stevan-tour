ITINERARY:

BALI → LABUAN BAJO → KELOR → MENJERITE → STRAWBERRY ROCK → KALONG ISLAND(L/D)

DAY 1 : Bali – Labuan bajo – Kelor – Menjerite – Strawberry rock – Kalong Island (L/D)

Your adventure begins early in the morning and get early flight from Bali to Labuan Bajo. Upon arrival, our team will warmly welcome you at the airport and transfer you directly to the harbor where your boat is ready.

Next, the boat heads to the unique Strawberry Rock, a round rock formation that resembles a strawberry from afar—an excellent spot for photos.

As the sun sets, you will arrive at Kalong Island, where thousands of flying foxes soar over the mangrove forest, creating one of the most magical sunsets in Komodo. Enjoy dinner on the boat and relax under a starry sky.

DAY 2 : Padar Island – Pink Beach – Komodo Island – Taka Makassar (B/L/D)

Wake up early to climb the iconic viewpoint of Padar Island. Sunrise from Padar is one of the most stunning in Indonesia, with three differently colored bays stretching in front of you. Afterward, sail to the famous Pink Beach, where the soft pink sands and turquoise waters invite you to swim, snorkel, or just relax.

Continue to Komodo Island, where you will trek with a certified ranger to observe the legendary Komodo Dragons in their natural habitat—one of the world’s last living prehistoric animals.

The final stop of the day is Taka Makassar, a crescent-shaped sandbar surrounded by shallow crystal-clear water. It’s a perfect place for photos and gentle swimming.

DAY 3 : Manta Point – Siaba Island – Gili Lawa (B/L/D)

Today you will encounter the graceful giants of the sea. Your first stop is Manta Point, where manta rays often gather to feed and glide effortlessly underwater. Snorkeling here gives you a chance to witness them up close.

Next, visit Siaba Island, also known as “Turtle City.” This area is home to many green turtles, making it an enjoyable and relaxing snorkeling experience.

In the afternoon, sail toward Gili Lawa, a dramatic island offering one of the best sunset treks in the park. Climb to the viewpoint for sweeping views of golden hills and deep blue waters.

DAY 4 : Kanawa Island – Return to Labuan Bajo (B/L)

After breakfast, make your final snorkeling stop at Kanawa Island, famous for its white beaches and vibrant coral reefs just a few meters from the shore.

Around midday, the boat will return to Labuan Bajo. Upon arrival, check in to your hotel and enjoy free time to relax, explore local cafés, or watch the sunset from the harbor.

DAY 5 : Kanawa Island – Return to Labuan Bajo (B/L)

After breakfast, your journey continues by car toward Denge Village, the starting point of the Wae Rebo trek. The drive takes around 4–5 hours through scenic mountains and villages.

Upon arrival in Denge, enjoy lunch at a local restaurant before beginning the 2.5–3.5-hour trek through lush forest and mountain trails.

As you arrive in Wae Rebo, you will be welcomed with a traditional Wae Lu’u ceremony inside one of the cone-shaped houses, called Mbaru Niang. Spend the evening interacting with the local community, learning their traditions, and enjoying a simple homemade dinner. Overnight in the traditional house of Wae Rebo.

DAY 6 : Wae Rebo – Denge – Labuan Bajo → Bali (B/L)

Wake up early to witness the magical morning mist surrounding Wae Rebo village. After breakfast, you have free time to explore, take photos, or visit the coffee plantations that the village is known for.

Begin the trek back to Denge, followed by lunch on the way. Our team will then drive you back to Labuan Bajo Airport for your afternoon flight back to Bali.

Your journey ends with beautiful memories of Komodo, the ocean, and the highland culture of Wae Rebo.
