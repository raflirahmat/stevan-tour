Heaven Gate and Ancient Vilage

10 hours • Pickup From Bali Area

Step into Bali’s spiritual and historical heart with the Heaven Gate and Ancient Village Tour. This journey takes you to Lempuyang Temple, famously known as Heaven Gate, where you can capture breathtaking panoramic views.   • Experience the ancient traditions of Tenganan Village, one of Bali’s oldest indigenous settlements
