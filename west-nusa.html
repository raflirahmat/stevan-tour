WEST NUSA PENIDA TOUR

This trip will visit:

Kelingking beach

Crystal bay

Broken beach

Angel billabong

Pick up hotel in sanur at 7.15 am or in kuta at 6.30 am. Departing from Sanur to Nusa Penida with the fast boat to nusa penida for 40 minutes.

Arrive in nusa penida at 9 am, then by car you will explore to the west of nusa penida island. Firstly, visit Kelingking beach. This place has beautiful nature view and completely showing you the amazing cliff which flank one small white sand beach. The formation of its cliffs seems like little finger form. So we call this in our language is kelingking beach or the name is taken from the shape itself. Next, visit broken beach.

So we call this in our language is kelingking beach or the name is taken from the shape itself. Next, visit broken beach. The broken beach uniquely has small circle beach and the sea water supplying to that beach with through the small hole under the land.

Next, visit angel billabong. This is near by the broken beach. It is small pool just located under rock and front of the lip of the sea. When high tide, the pool would be had much water, you could swim to the pool. Next, visit crystal. This beach offer beautiful white sand beach with water and rich with under water ecosystem, such as, kinds of fishes and corals. At 4.30 pm back to sanur with public fast boat. arrive in sanur, we drop off hotel, such as sanur area, kuta, seminyak, jimbaran and nusa dua.
