ITINERARY:

DAY 1 – Labuan Bajo → Denge Village → Trek to Wae Rebo (L/D)

Your journey starts with a morning pick-up from your hotel in Labuan Bajo. After meeting our guide, you will begin the scenic 4–5 hour drive toward Denge Village, passing lush hills, local villages, and beautiful mountain landscapes.

Upon arrival in Denge, enjoy lunch at a local restaurant before beginning the 2.5–3.5 hour trek through forested hills and mountain paths. The trail is refreshing with cool air, birdsong, and natural surroundings.

In the evening, enjoy a warm, simple dinner prepared by the local community. Spend the night in the traditional house, sharing space just as the locals do—an authentic cultural experience.

Overnight in Wae Rebo (Traditional House).

DAY 2 – Wae Rebo → Denge → Labuan Bajo (B/L)

Wake up early to enjoy the sunrise, often with mist covering the village for a magical morning atmosphere. After breakfast, you can explore the village, meet the locals, visit the coffee plantation, and capture stunning photographs of the iconic circular village.

Late morning, begin the descent back to Denge Village. Lunch will be served at a local restaurant before returning to Labuan Bajo.

Arrive back in town in the afternoon and transfer to your hotel.

End of Tour.
