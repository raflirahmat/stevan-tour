Uluwatu and Kecak Fire Dance

10 hours • Pickup From Bali Area

Experience the cultural and natural wonders of Bali with our Uluwatu Temple & Kecak Dance Tour. This journey takes you through majestic temples, pristine beaches, and a mesmerizing fire dance performance set against the backdrop of a stunning cliffside sunset. Visit the impressive Garuda Wisnu Kencana (GWK) Cultural Park, relax at the scenic Padang Padang Beach, witness the iconic Uluwatu Temple, and end the evening with a traditional Kecak Dance performance, followed by a romantic seafood dinner at Jimbaran Beach.

Highlights:

  • Explore Garuda Wisnu Kencana (GWK) Cultural Park, home to the towering Wisnu statue
  • Relax on the white sands of Padang Padang Beach, a surfer’s paradise
  • Watch the breathtaking sunset at Uluwatu Temple, perched on a dramatic cliff
  • Witness the Kecak Fire Dance, an enchanting Balinese cultural performance
