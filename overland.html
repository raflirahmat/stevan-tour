FLORES OVERLAND 5 DAYS 4 NIGHTS

Explore the natural beauty, culture, and landscapes of Flores in this 5-day journey. From the mystical Kelimutu Lakes to traditional villages, spiderweb rice fields, and stunning coastlines—this overland tour is the perfect way to experience the heart of Flores.

ITINERARY:

DAY 1 – Arrival Ende → Moni Village

Upon your arrival at Ende Airport, our guide and driver will warmly welcome you. After a short briefing, begin the journey to Moni Village, a peaceful highland area at the foot of Mount Kelimutu.

On the way, visit:

  • • Wologai Traditional Village, famous for its traditional Lio architecture.
  • • Local market or natural viewpoints depending on timing.

Arrive at Moni, check in to your mountain lodge, and enjoy the cool evening atmosphere.

DAY 2 – Sunrise Kelimutu → Blue Stone Beach → Riung

Wake up early (around 04:00 AM) and drive to Kelimutu National Park. Trek for 20–25 minutes to the summit to witness sunrise over the Three-Colored Crater Lakes, one of the most iconic views in Indonesia.

Return to the lodge for breakfast, then continue the drive toward Riung, located on the northern coast.

Stops along the way:

  • • Nanga Penda Blue Stone Beach (Pantai Batu Biru).
  • •Scenic coastal roads & small traditional villages.

Arrive in Riung and check in.

DAY 3 – Riung 17 Islands Marine Park → Bajawa

After breakfast, head to the harbor and take a wooden boat to explore the peaceful 17 Islands Marine Park.

Activities include:

  • • Snorkeling in crystal-clear waters
  • • Checking the flying fox island (thousands of fruit bats)
  • • Swimming & relaxing on white-sand beaches

Enjoy lunch BBQ-style on one of the islands.

In the afternoon, drive to Bajawa, a cool mountain town known for its culture and hot springs. Check in and rest.

DAY 4 – Bena Village → Aimere → Ranamese Lake → Ruteng

Start the day by visiting Bena Traditional Village, one of the oldest and most well-preserved Megalithic villages in Flores. Explore the traditional houses, stone altars, and local weaving.

Next stops:

  • • Malangga Hot Springs (optional stop for relaxing)
  • • Aimere village, known for its traditional arak distillation
  • • Ranamese Crater Lake, surrounded by lush forest

Continue to Ruteng, a cool mountain town famous for its agriculture and Catholic heritage.

DAY 5 – Cancar Spiderweb Rice Fields → Labuan Bajo

After breakfast, visit the famous Lingko Spiderweb Rice Fields in Cancar. This unique land division system, shaped like a spiderweb, is found only in Manggarai culture.

Continue the scenic drive to Labuan Bajo, passing mountains, valleys, and coastline views.

Arrive in Labuan Bajo around afternoon. Transfer to your hotel or airport.
